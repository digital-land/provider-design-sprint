Planning data providers – prototype

This is a prototype of the Planning Data provider's service. It is not a live service and is not intended for use in production. The prototype is being used to test the design and functionality of the service with users.

The service is being developed by the Digital Planning team at the Ministry of Housing, Communities and Local Government (MHCLG).
